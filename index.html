About game

In the blockchain game DMNTR on planet Cyberia, players play on Dominators, creatures that absorb energon energy. They face dangers in deep caves guarded by robots. Once activated, Dominators unleash powerful energy, enabling players to create robot workers. However, as the robots multiply, players must control their numbers using special programs and deploy security robots to maintain balance and safety, mastering the game's challenges while harnessing the game's unique resource, energon.

FAQ

01. What is DOMINATOR?
DOMINATOR is a revolutionary blockchain-powered gaming platform that offers players immersive gameplay experiences, NFT integration, decentralized finance (DeFi) mechanics, and opportunities to earn rewards. It leverages blockchain technology to redefine the gaming landscape, providing unparalleled control over in-game assets and enhanced security.
02. What sets DOMINATOR apart from other crypto games?
DOMINATOR stands out for its unique gameplay mechanics, including player-versus-player battles, NFT marketplace, staking, and play-to-earn features. With seamless integration of smart contracts, NFTs, and AI-driven gameplay, DOMINATOR offers players unprecedented control, security, and opportunities for earning rewards.
03. How can I participate in DOMINATOR?
To participate in DOMINATOR, simply create an account on the platform's website and start exploring the gaming ecosystem. Engage in thrilling PVP battles, trade NFTs on the marketplace, stake tokens for rewards, and earn rewards through play-to-earn mechanics. Whether you're a player, developer, investor, or advertiser, there's a role for everyone in the DOMINATOR ecosystem.
04. What is the revenue model for DOMINATOR?
DOMINATOR generates revenue through various channels, including transaction fees, advertising revenue, marketplace fees, and revenue from championships and tournaments with additional prize pools. Additionally, the platform offers opportunities for investors to participate in revenue sharing, staking, and governance decisions.
05. How can I convert money into and out of DOMINATOR platform, and which currencies are supported?
To convert stable coin (USDT) into DOMINATOR (DMD) tokens, users can utilize our cryptocurrency exchanges where DMD you can transfer in or out easy. Additionally, users can participate in token sales and events hosted by the DOMINATOR platform to acquire DMD tokens. Conversely, users can convert DMD tokens back into fiat currency or other cryptocurrencies by selling them on supported exchanges. Supported currencies for conversion may include major cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), as well as stable coins like USDT, USDC, and others.
